Faith in Jesus Christ

Faith in Jesus Christ is the first principle of the gospel. It is a firm belief that He is the Son of God and the Savior of the world.

Repentance

Repentance is the process of turning away from sin and turning towards God. It includes feeling sorrow for sin, confessing sins, and making restitution where possible.

Baptism

Baptism is the first ordinance of the gospel. It is performed by immersion in water and is necessary for salvation.

Gift of the Holy Ghost

The gift of the Holy Ghost is received after baptism through the laying on of hands. It provides guidance, comfort, and sanctification.

Endure to the End

Enduring to the end means continually following the path of righteousness throughout our lives, remaining faithful to the covenants we have made.
